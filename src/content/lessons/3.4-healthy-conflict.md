# Healthy Conflict

## Introduction
If everyone always agrees, someone is lying. Healthy conflict is how bad ideas are filtered out and good ideas are strengthened. The goal is not to avoid conflict, but to handle it without destroying relationships.

## Core Concepts

### 1. SBI Model (Feedback)
When you need to have a hard conversation, use **S-B-I**:
- **Situation**: "During the morning standup..." (Fact)
- **Behavior**: "...you interrupted me three times..." (Fact)
- **Impact**: "...which made me feel unheard and less likely to share my ideas." (Result)
- **Wait**: Then listen.

### 2. "I" Statements
- **Bad**: "You are lazy." (Accusatory, attacks identity).
- **Good**: "I feel stressed when the files are late because I have to work overtime." (Descriptive, focuses on impact).
